Refund Policy

At Epitome IAS, we strive to provide the best learning experience. However, if you wish to request a refund, please review our policy below.

Refund requests must be made within 7 days of enrollment. To be eligible, the student must attend all classes scheduled for the day.

Refunds will be processed after deducting applicable processing fees. The amount will be credited to the original payment method within the standard processing time.

Refund Process:

  1. Submit a refund request through your student portal or by contacting our support team.
  2. Our team will review your request within 48 hours.
  3. If approved, the refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is processed.

Non-Refundable Items:

  • Study materials that have been accessed or downloaded
  • Registration fees
  • Administrative charges

For assistance, please contact our Support Team at support@epitomeias.in or call us at +91 9876543210.

Note: Epitome IAS reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website.
